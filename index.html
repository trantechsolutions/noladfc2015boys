About New Orleans DFC

DFC is a youth soccer club based in Kenner, LA. DFC’s mission is to create a positive impact on youth in our community through quality soccer training, thereby improving opportunities for players of all ages and playing abilities to create a passion for the sport of soccer that will last a lifetime, and to help raise the level of soccer in our community. Inherent in this mission are the goals of providing youth development programs and promoting pay-less-to-play programs for competitive teams.

DFC has adopted the following Core Values which are an integral part of our Club’s operations. These values set the standards for behavior and performance of everyone. Players and Coaches are asked to learn and live the Core Values to help them become better players and better people.

At DFC, we believe the soccer experience should not be defined by wins, goals, championships, etc., rather by the impact we leave on people that will hopefully last a lifetime.

Meet the Boys

New Orleans DFC 2015 Boys is a group of ambitious and motivated players who are working hard to develop more professionalism, structure, and ultimately a higher level of training, which translates to better and more successful match day experiences. The main objective of their program is to create confident, brave, creative, and problem-solving players, focusing on individual player improvement, maximizing technical ability, ball mastery, and game-related decision making.

Our Goals

EXCELLENCE

We understand that we will not achieve the impact we seek without sustained dedication. We must be disciplined in terms of becoming the best people we can be.

PERSEVERANCE

Setbacks are inevitable, but our response defines us. We face every challenge with resilience, learn from defeat, and push forward with unwavering determination.

PASSION

Our love for the game is the fuel that drives us. It's in every practice and every match, inspiring us to be better and to ignite that same fire in our teammates.

SPORTSMANSHIP

We compete with integrity and honor. This means respecting the rules, our opponents, officials, and each other. True victory lies in conducting ourselves with class and dignity.

SERVICE

Our responsibility extends beyond the soccer field. We are dedicated to making a positive impact in our community, using our platform to serve others and strengthen the bonds that connect us all.
